Crew — powered by RaceDay

Everything your crew needs to get your runner to the finish line.

Crew is RaceDay's planning and collaboration app for ultramarathon support crews. Assign roles, map every aid station, coordinate pacers, and stay in sync on race day — so your crew can focus on what it does best: keeping your runner moving forward.

Roles & Team

One crew, every role covered.

Add every member of your crew and assign the roles that matter on race day. Crew Chief, Pacer, Crew Member — one person can wear more than one hat, and everyone works off the same plan.

  • Add crew members in seconds
  • Assign Crew Chief, Pacer, or Crew Member roles
  • One person, multiple roles — no problem
  • Every member sees the same up-to-date plan

Route & Aid Stations

Every aid station, mapped and ready.

Upload your runner's race GPX and the course maps itself. Drop a pin for every aid station, assign a pacer to each one, and know exactly when your crew needs to be where — down to the food, drop bags, and mantras waiting at every stop.

  • Upload race GPX to auto-plot the course
  • Drop a pin for every aid station
  • Assign a pacer to each stop with estimated times
  • Food, drop bag instructions, and team mantras per station

Live Tracking & Communication

Stay connected, mile after mile.

Track your runner as the miles tick by and send updates the moment they're needed. Runners can call or text the Crew Chief directly, and every time and distance update helps the whole crew recalculate finish time together.

  • Live run tracking with automatic updates
  • A direct line to the Crew Chief, anytime
  • Log time & distance to estimate finish
  • Bulk-message the whole crew in one tap

Shared Memories

Turn the miles into a memory that lasts.

Every member of the crew can drop photos and videos into a shared, chronological timeline as race day unfolds. The result is a "Type 2 Fun Memory" — the kind of story the whole team gets to relive for years after the finish line.

  • A shared photo & video timeline for race day
  • Every crew member can contribute, in real time
  • Relive the race together, long after it's over
